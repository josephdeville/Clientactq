**Competitive Dynamics**:
- Media Composer maintains share in broadcast/post-production but declining in commercial/corporate
- Adobe Premiere growing market share with Creative Cloud bundling advantage
- DaVinci Resolve disrupting with freemium model and color grading integration
- Subscription transition critical to compete with Adobe's recurring revenue model

- **Ross Video** - Newsroom systems and production automation
